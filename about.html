Founder, designer, and only full-time conversion person at WebThangs. Based in Lisbon. Ships from anywhere with espresso.

I started WebThangs after watching agencies spend six months redesigning a website that, on the day it shipped, converted worse than the one it replaced. Beautiful didn't mean useful. Modern didn't mean clear. Award-winning didn't mean leads.

Now the studio runs on three rules. One: every change has a hypothesis. Two: every hypothesis has a measurement. Three: if it doesn't move the number, we kill it — even if it looks great.

We work with service businesses, B2B SaaS, and e-commerce brands who'd rather have a website that converts than a website that wins design awards. (Though sometimes — happily — it does both.)
